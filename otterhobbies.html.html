What do otters do in their free time?

Swimming is a large part of their lives as they have uniquely webbed feet. Adorable.

But they never get wet, their hair actually traps warm air underneath the layer of wet fur, keeping them warm and insulated.

On occasion you can spot a otter making a water slide and sliding down it for nothing but enjoyment. Scientists agree that there is no survival reason for the slides, it is created by the otter just for the fun of the otter, even in the winter!.

They also spend time collecting and playing with small stones. Look at those tiny lil' paws.
