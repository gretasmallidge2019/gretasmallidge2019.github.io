A Whole Page About Otters!

Hello! This page is dedicated to the super underrated otter. Otters have several different species, are adorable, enjoy many hobbies as well as many different delicacies.

Otters of the mammalia family, the most common and familiar otter to us is the North American River Otter, which is a smaller otter. Otters generally find their homes in dens called a holt or a couch. That's right, otters like couches.
